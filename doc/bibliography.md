## The Light Transport Problems

Two groups of scene objects:
- primary source (light emitters)
- secondary source (light reflection, refraction and scattering)

- scope of the simulation : geometric, wave or quantum optics
- phenomena
  - light emission
  - diffuse reflection
  - specular reflection
  - refraction
  - absorbtion
  - participating media
  - continuously varying refractive index
  - monochromatic vs polychromatic light
  - light transmission
  - light dispersion
  - ideal specular scattering
  - ideal diffuse (reflection or transmission)
